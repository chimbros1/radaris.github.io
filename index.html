RADARIS

Frontal Collision Warning System for Low Visibility

Project Overview

Radaris is a specialized safety system designed to reduce road accidents in conditions of poor visibility, such as fog, rain, and snow[cite: 2]. Using radio signals instead of visual sensors, it ensures effective accident prevention regardless of weather or terrain[cite: 3, 13].

Range

Detects threats up to 900 meters away[cite: 15].

Technology

Built on ATmega 328 and NRF 2.4 GHz wireless modules[cite: 9, 47].

Reliability

Independent of weather conditions and external lighting[cite: 13, 37].
